About

Welcome to the Museum of Candy!Cocoa! Our museum is a sweet and interactive experience for visitors of all ages.

Step inside and be transported to a world of delight, with exhibits showcasing the history and evolution of candychocolate, imaginative and immersive displays, and special themed rooms filled with wondrous treats.

Tickets

To purchase tickets, you can reserve online, or purchase in person at the museum on the day of your visit. We offer individual tickets, group rates, and special discount rates.

We recommend purchasing tickets in advance, as the museum can get busy during peak times. We look forward to seeing you at the Museum of Candy!Cocoa!
